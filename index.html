Controls

W,A,S,D, Enter: cast spell, Shift: Select spell, Space: Jump

In the school use the mouse to select the spell you want to buy.

Instructions

The game is over if you run out of mana (measured in $). Avoid homeless guys that will take your money. Trade stuff (casting spell) with nice citiziens until you suck them dry. Attend to the business school for wizards and learn new spells.
